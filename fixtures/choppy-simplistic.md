# The Cat

The cat sat. The cat is big. The cat is red. It ran. It ran fast. It ate. It ate
a lot. The dog ran too. The dog is small. The dog is fun. They played. They had
fun. It was a good day. The end was near. The end came. They went home. They ate.
They slept. It was late. The sun was down. The stars were out. It was dark. They
were tired. They rested. It was quiet. The night was long. Morning came. They woke.
